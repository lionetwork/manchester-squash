Coach's advice

Not sure which racket to choose?

Beginners

Look for a lighter, head-balanced racket that's forgiving on off-centre hits. You don't need to spend a fortune to get a frame that will serve you well while you learn.

Juniors

Junior rackets are shorter and lighter, sized for growing players. The right size makes a huge difference to technique. Ask me what suits your child's age and build.

Improvers & club players

As your swing develops, balance, weight and string setup start to matter. Bring your game to a session and I'll happily recommend a frame to match how you play.

Unsure? Just ask at your next session, or get in touch. I'll point you at the right racket before you spend a penny.
