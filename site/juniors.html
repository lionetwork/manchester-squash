Junior squash coaching

Building physical fitness and mental robustness in young players through the sport of squash.

Junior programme

Fun first, skills for life

The coaching of juniors receives special attention at Manchester Squash. We strive to assist the younger generation to develop both physical fitness and mental robustness through squash, a sport often called physical chess.

We have structured programmes designed to improve squash skills, with fitness drills and games to ensure kids have fun and also develop skills suitable for other sports.

  • Junior classes and one-on-one lessons available weekly
  • Junior fees start from just £15
  • Holiday camps at Grove Park Squash Club
  • DBS-checked coach with current First Aid and Child Protection Safeguarding
  • Volunteer committee member of the Manchester Junior League
