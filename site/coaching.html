Schools partnership

Taster squash sessions for schools

We are currently inviting schools in the North West to take part in taster squash sessions for children aged 6–12. The sessions can be conducted in schools using a specially designed mini squash court.

This scheme provides an opportunity for boys and girls to take part in fitness-based squash activity under the guidance of qualified England Squash coaches, organised in partnership with local clubs, squash coaches and leisure centres.

The taster sessions give children an opportunity to play and learn in an organised setting while being introduced to a sport that will be new to many of them. Following the taster sessions, there is the opportunity to discuss further sessions, either in addition to, or as part of, PE activities within your school curriculum.

We would appreciate an early indication of your interest so we can make the necessary arrangements: call 07941 778 466 or email manchestersquash@gmail.com.
