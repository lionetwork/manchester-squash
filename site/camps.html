Camps

Holiday squash camps

Action-packed junior squash camps at Grove Park Squash Club during the school holidays.

What to expect

A full programme of squash and fun

Our junior squash camps run at Grove Park Squash Club during the school holidays. Each camp combines coaching, match play, fitness drills and games. It is the perfect way for juniors to make big strides in their game while having a great time with other young players.

  • Run by an England Squash qualified professional coach
  • Suitable for a range of ages and abilities
  • Skills, drills, matches and prizes
  • DBS-checked, First Aid and safeguarding trained
