[h4] Schools Partnership 
[p] We currently inviting schools in the Northwest to take part in Taster Squash Sessions (children from 6-12 years old) the sessions can be conducted in schools using a specially designed mini squash court. 
[p] This scheme will provide an opportunity for boys and girls to take part in fitness based squash sports activity under the guidance of qualified England Squash Coaches. This new initiative is organised in partnership with local clubs, squash coaches and leisure centers. 
[p] The taster sessions will give the children an opportunity to play and learn in an organised setting, while at the same time introducing to them a sport that will be new to many of them. Following the taster sessions, there will be the opportunity to discuss the possibility of further sessions, either in addition to, or as part of your P E activities within your school curriculum. 
[p] We would appreciate an early indication of your interest in taking part in the taster sessions so that we can make necessary arrangements.
