[h2] Why Manchester Squash? 
[li] Understanding the rules of the game of Squash. 
[li] Specific footwork and Techniques of hitting the squash ball. 
[li] Developing Good Timing Tactics and Strategy. 
[li] Fitness Training and Psychology learning to compete effectively safely. 
[li] All of this taught by a seasoned pro who still competes at the highest level. 
[li] Squash develops good cardiovascular fitness, sharpens reflexes, improves mobility and mental toughness.
